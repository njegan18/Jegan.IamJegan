What is wisdom

how can I gain wisdom. ? Is worship my god. I read and follow my holy religious books. Can I assume that would gain wisdom ? Every Individual is different, everyone has their own journey and path way. Getting knowledge from one specific religion or sector will not be sufficient. Worshipping Christ, Krishna or Allah and reading those holy books and following alone will not gain wisdom. One should have principles of his own after understanding the good, bad, nature of life and whatever comes in his way, should be determined in following it. The determination comes from mind, and if one is capable of controlling the mind and not controlled by mind, should be able to follow the paths of renowned saints or even creates his own foot path.
